About JobRec

JobRec is a comprehensive job access and recommendation system designed to connect job seekers with their ideal opportunities.

Features

  • Personalized Recommendations: Get job suggestions based on your skills and experience
  • Easy Application: Apply to jobs with one click
  • Job Bookmarks: Save jobs to review later
  • Application Tracking: Keep track of your job applications
  • Notifications: Stay updated on your application status

For Employers

Our admin dashboard allows you to post jobs, manage applications, and find the best candidates for your positions.
